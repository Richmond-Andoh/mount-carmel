5. FERTILITY CENTRE SPECIALIZATIONS
Advanced Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) services:
- In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF): Egg retrieval, fertilization, embryo culture, and transfer.
- Intrauterine Insemination (IUI): Sperm preparation and timed insemination.
- Fertility Assessment: Hormone testing, semen analysis, ovulation tracking, tubal patency.
- Fertility Preservation: Egg, sperm, and embryo freezing.
- Donor Programs: Screening and matching for donor eggs, sperm, and embryos.
- Counseling: Professional emotional and psychological support for couples.
- High Success Rates: Proven track record with state-of-the-art equipment.

6. MATERNITY & NEONATAL SERVICES
- Prenatal Care: Regular checkups, ultrasound scans, blood tests, and nutrition counseling.
- Delivery options: Natural delivery, Cesarean section (C-Section), and specialized pain management.
- Postnatal Care: Newborn care, breastfeeding support, and recovery monitoring.
- High-Risk Pregnancy: Specialized fetal monitoring and emergency interventions.
- Neonatal ICU (NICU): 24/7 intensive care for premature or critical newborns.
- Family Planning: Comprehensive counseling and reproductive health assistance.

7. LABORATORY & DIAGNOSTIC CAPABILITIES
Specialized testing with quick turnaround times:
- Blood Tests: CBC, Lipid Panel, Liver Function, Kidney Function.
- Microbiology: Cultures, sensitivity testing, and infectious disease diagnostics.
- Cardiac Markers: Troponin, CK-MB, BNP for heart health.
- Hormone Testing: Thyroid function, reproductive hormones, insulin, cortisol.
- Tumor Markers: Cancer screenings (PSA, CEA, CA 125, AFP).
- Immunology: Autoantibody testing, allergy testing, rheumatoid factor.
- Imaging: Advanced X-rays and Ultrasounds.

8. SURGERY, ORTHOPEDICS & PEDIATRICS
- Surgical Department: Performs emergency and elective surgeries including laparoscopy.
- Orthopedics: Handles fractures, joint replacements, and sports medicine.
- Pediatrics: Offers well-child visits, vaccinations, and growth monitoring from newborns to adolescents.
